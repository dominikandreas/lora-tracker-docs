P0 release blockers

  1. Authenticated encryption and replay-safe ACKs for LoRa.
  2. Authenticated BLE provisioning and protected key bootstrap.
  3. Signed OTA with Secure Boot, flash/NVS encryption and key custody.
  4. Durable gateway buffering with broker-confirmed delivery.
  5. Current gateway hardware port and hardware-in-the-loop CI.
  6. Power, RF, environmental and multi-day recovery qualification.
  7. Monitoring, backup/restore, credential rotation and compliance evidence.

Controlled field-trial gate

Use a private TLS broker and isolated network, unique credentials, attended provisioning and no public HTTP/BLE exposure. Assume LoRa traffic can be observed or forged. Back up history, document device revisions and keep an independent animal-location/safety plan.
