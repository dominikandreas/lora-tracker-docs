P0 release blockers

  1. Signed OTA with ESP Secure Boot, flash/NVS encryption and documented production key custody.
  2. A wear-levelled, power-loss-safe tracker queue instead of RTC-only unacknowledged history.
  3. Radio/admin key rotation, revocation, recovery and protected fleet bootstrap tooling.
  4. Current gateway hardware port and automated hardware-in-the-loop validation.
  5. Power, RF, environmental and multi-day outage/recovery qualification.
  6. Monitoring, backup/restore, incident response and compliance evidence.

Controlled field-trial gate

Use a private TLS broker and isolated network, unique credentials, attended key transfer and no public HTTP/BLE exposure. Treat radio confidentiality as defense in depth until the key lifecycle and device boot chain are qualified. Back up history, document device revisions and keep an independent animal-location/safety plan.
